God of war

God of War is a hack and slash video game franchise created by SCE Santa Monica Studio and distributed by Sony Computer Entertainment. It is based on the adventures of a Spartan demigod, Kratos, who faces various characters from Greek and Norse mythology, both heroes (Heracles, Theseus, Perseus, etc.); mythological species (gorgons, harpies, or minotaurs); Greek gods (Ares, Poseidon, Zeus, among others), titans (such as Cronos) and primordial gods (such as Gaia). Although the Spartan warrior is accustomed to antagonize most of the gods, he receives help from many of them at some point in each game, especially from Athena.
